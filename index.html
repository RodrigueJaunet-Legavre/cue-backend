How it works

SIMPLE.
POWERFUL.

Two worlds, one platform. Whether you're a DJ or a venue, CUE makes the connection seamless.

For DJs
01

Create your profile

Build a professional DJ profile with your mixes, genres, and biography. Get verified by our team.

02

Receive offers

Venues discover your profile and send booking offers directly through our secure messaging system.

03

Get paid securely

Sign AI-generated contracts, perform your set, and receive your payment — fully protected by CUE.

01
